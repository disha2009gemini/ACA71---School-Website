Campus Life


At Ekya, we understand that social connections are key to the wellbeing of our children. To support the social and emotional needs of our students by staying socially connected with their peers even during physical isolation, we planned several activities such as school bands, student led events (intra-school fests, quizzing, debates, etc.), small student group talk sessions and more!

What's new?

Aryaman is a marathon for students of Ekya from Grade 1-10 to get togethor and run for a cause with friends and family by their side. There are various categories such as 1km, 3km and 5km. This cause will help students empathize with the weaker sections of the soceity, help raise money for them as well as to have some fun! Anybody can register for the mass event conducted through the prime areas of South Bengaluru. The more the merrier!
